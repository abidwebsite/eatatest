ETEA Exam Instructions

General Guidelines

  • Ensure you arrive at the exam center at least 30 minutes early.
  • Bring your admission card and a valid form of identification.
  • Do not bring any electronic devices, including mobile phones, into the exam room.
  • Follow all instructions given by the invigilators.

Exam Format

The ETEA exam consists of multiple-choice questions covering various subjects. Make sure to review the syllabus before the exam date.

Scoring System

Each correct answer earns points, while incorrect answers may result in negative marking. Please check the official guidelines for specific scoring details.

After the Exam

  • Results will be announced on the official ETEA website.
  • Keep your admission card for future reference regarding your results.
